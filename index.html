About Us

NaR offers vegetation control services and management solutions for industrial, landscape, forestry, and agricultural settings across the Peace Country. Owners Riley Nooy and Nick Boivin have extensive combined experience in vegetation management, oil and gas, and agriculture industries.

We rely on following an IPM (Integrated Pest Management) plan for our customers; this ensures a proactive approach to vegetation management and assists in a long-term solution. This long-term solution will create better relationships with landowners and government regulators.

We look forward to building and maintaining relationships with both employees and customers, for long term vegetation management solutions. With our main headquarters located 20 Minutes North West of Spirit River (Blueberry Mountain), AB, NaR is perfectly situated in the heart of the peace country to easily assist you in your vegetation management needs.
